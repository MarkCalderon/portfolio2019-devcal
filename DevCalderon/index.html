A short bio about myself

I started my self as an information communications and technology teacher. During the course of the years, I have learnt to adapt and foresee mistakes as a learning opportunity to better myself. As an educator, time is a luxury to have, and so practising time management and proper planning is one of the goals I strive for each day. I myself being a Computer Science and Software Engineering graduate we were taught to consider the life cycle of a project. Each cycle needs time to reflect, a time to communicate, and

a time for learning.
